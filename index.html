2020-12-26
Biology

Water Properties 1. Polarity What is it? Water molecules are polar. It made of 2 atoms of hydrogen, partialy positive charge and 1 atoms of oxygen, partialy negative charge. These atoms are hold together by Covalent Bond. Electrons gather with the oxygen atom making it Electronegative. Two different water molecule connect to one another via Hydrogen Bong, where an atom of oxygen from one water molecule attracts to a hydrogen atom of another water molecule.
